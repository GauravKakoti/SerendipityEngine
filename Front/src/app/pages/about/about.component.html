About the Serendipity Engine

When people try to orient themselves in a specific city, they use - what’s called - a mental map. This is a map representing your view of that city. With the serendipity engine, we try to surprise people with a new point of view on their city by changing this mental map.

Do you think you would recognize your city when it is put upside down? Or do you really know every interesting spot? Give our serendipity engine a try and find out new ways of looking at a map!

Don’t forget to share your awesome design.
The serendipity engine is a collaboration project between IDLab, imec and Open Summer of Code.
